The Waukesha Chess Club is open!

Come on out on Wednesdays and join us.

A new event - The Early Spring Swiss - started recently on Wed, 4/9 - a regular old 4 round G/100d5 Swiss in two sections.
It's not too late to enter. Check the blog or let one of the TDs know if you want to join, or just to get more information

We recently completed our 2025 Club Blitz Event on 4/2 - Congratulations to ??? (have to get the details from Jim and Kevin...)!

We recently completed our 2025 Club Championship on 3/26 - Congratulations to Evan Seghers!

Nearly all events are USCF-rated

For newcomers to rated chess:
- You will need to be a US Chess Federation (USCF) member.
- You will almost certainly need to know how to use a clock and keep score.
If any of this is confusing, we can get you sorted properly, just ask!!
Typically, our only unrated events are our twice-yearly blitz event and our Chess in the Park club summer outing.

Any Questions??

Feel free to send an email to one of the TDs and we'll get you set up.
Hope to see you soon at the Eagle's Club in Waukesha on Wednesdays!!

Health and Safety Information

The health and safety of our members are paramount to the Waukesha Chess Club. Everyone should use their best judgement and decide each Wednesday evening whether or not it is safe for them and others to come to the club that night.
